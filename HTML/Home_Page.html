Sidi history

More than 25+ years of experience

Welcome to Sidi, where the ocean's bounty meets culinary mastery. For over 25 years, Sidi has been a beacon of excellence in seafood dining, offering a tantalizing array of dishes that showcase the freshest catches from the sea.

Nestled in the heart of Alex, Sidi is a celebration of coastal flavors and timeless traditions. With a deep-rooted passion for seafood and a commitment to quality, we curate an unforgettable dining experience that transports you to the shores with every bite.
